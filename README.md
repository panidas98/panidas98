## 👋 About Me

Business Intelligence Analyst with **8+ years of experience** turning data into competitive advantage. I've led and coordinated BI teams across the **energy, engineering, AI, and BPO** sectors, driving data-informed decisions across the organization.

I work with **Power BI, Grafana, SQL, Python, and AWS**, with strong experience in database administration, data warehouses, and data lakes. I specialize in **ETL, data governance, and data quality**, applied to sales, inventory, customer behavior, financial performance (P&L, profitability), and customer satisfaction analysis.
